What is a Rube-Goldberg Machine?

A Rube-Goldberg machine is a combonation of many small contraptions, gadgets, and pieces, that make up a larger machine that only needs one beginning source of input to take place for the string of machines to start to work.

It was first created by Rube Goldberg, who is a Pulitzer Prize winning cartoonist.
